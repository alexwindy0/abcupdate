Our story: Building a Marketplace, Together

Asaba Business Community was established by Ada Onwubiko in 2020 with the mission of empowering local businesses by providing opportunities to increase sales, build valuable connections, and collaborate effectively.

Our platform is designed to help you get referrals and close deals without the need to personally know anyone in Asaba, Nigeria. Since our inception, we've evolved into a dynamic organization, now supported by a talented team of six members, each bringing unique skills to the table. We've grown to become a key resource for up to 1,000 business owners in Asaba, offering a strong network that fosters growth and success. regularly host physical events, with some attracting over 400 entrepreneurs in one setting, providing unmatched opportunities for networking and business development. Asaba Business Community is your one-stop hub for accessing business opportunities and making meaningful connections in the city of Asaba.

We believe sustainable local economies start with strong, connected small businesses. From a monthly market day to a full-scale vendor expo and structured training series — our programs are built around measurable outcomes for members.

Our mission

We've grown to become a key resource for up to 1,000 business owners in Asaba Our mission is to empower small businesses in Asaba with market access, practical skills, and trusted partnerships so they can grow resilient, profitable enterprises.

Our vision

Our vision is to build a thriving business ecosystem that empowers our members to compete globally, reduce poverty, and achieve their full potential.

Objectives

  • Host regular markets and an annual expo to increase sales opportunities.
  • Deliver targeted training for business basics and digital payments.
  • Connect vendors to bulk buyers and procurement opportunities.

How we work

Our approach is practical and outcome-driven. We focus on three pillars:

  1. Accessible Markets: An annual vendor expo where vetted vendors meet paying customers and institutional buyers.
  2. Practical Training: Short, hands-on workshops on pricing, merchandising, digital payments, and basic bookkeeping — built for action, not theory.

Membership benefits

  • Priority stall booking for markets and the annual expo.
  • Access to business clinics and mentorship sessions.
  • Featured vendor listings for buyers and partners.

Milestones: 2021 — 2025

2021

Asaba Business Vendors Hangout 1.0

In 2021, we hosted a significant event where 150 business owners came together for our special hangout. This gathering was designed to foster connections, encourage collaboration, and provide ample networking opportunities among entrepreneurs in the city of Asaba. The event was a resounding success, offering a platform for participants to share ideas, explore potential partnerships, and build relationships that could propel their businesses forward. It was more than just a networking event; it was a space where local business owners could engage in meaningful discussions, exchange valuable insights, and strengthen the sense of community within the Asaba business landscape.

2022

Asaba Business Vendors Hangout 2.0

In 2022, we took our efforts to the next level by expanding our reach and hosting an even larger event with 300 business owners in attendance. This event not only provided a platform for networking, collaboration, and business growth but also recognized excellence within our community.We proudly awarded several vendors who had excelled in their respective fields, celebrating their achievements and contributions to the Asaba business ecosystem. These awards were a testament to the hard work and dedication of our members, highlighting the remarkable talent and innovation within our community. The event was a milestone in our journey, reinforcing our commitment to supporting and elevating local businesses. It was more than just a networking event; it was a space where local business owners could engage in meaningful discussions, exchange valuable insights, and strengthen the sense of community within the Asaba business landscape.

2023

Asaba Business Vendors Hangout 3.0

In 2023, we continued our growth trajectory by hosting our largest event yet, bringing together 356 vendors for an incredible day of connection, collaboration, and celebration. This event was not just about networking; it was a dynamic space where business owners could forge new partnerships, exchange ideas, and explore opportunities for mutual growth. In recognition of the dedication and hard work of our members, we once again took the time to honor those who had made significant strides in their industries. Awards were presented to outstanding vendors who had demonstrated exceptional performance and innovation in their fields. This recognition was a testament to the thriving entrepreneurial spirit within our community and reinforced our mission to support and uplift local businesses in Asaba. The 2023 event solidified our reputation as a cornerstone of business networking and development in the region, furthering our commitment to helping local entrepreneurs succeed.

2024

Asaba Business Vendors Hangout 4.0

In 2024, we took a big step forward by seeking partnerships and sponsorships to further support our community. We were thrilled to partner with well-known brands like Pancake Nigeria, Amadiva Bridals, BD Photons, Freshstart Interior Academy, IkeBest Technology, Grill House, Kidessence Kiddies Store, and Korrect Logistics. This year’s event was our largest yet, bringing together 400 business owners for a day of networking, collaboration, and celebration. One of the highlights was our business pitch competition, where three businesses that excelled in their presentations were awarded funds to help grow their ventures. We also recognized and rewarded the Entrepreneurs of the Year, honoring those who had shown exceptional innovation and consistent success throughout the previous year.

2025

Asaba Business Vendors Hangout 5.0

In 2025, the Business Culture Fest took on a whole new dimension. This year’s edition beautifully infused the richness of Nigerian culture, with vibrant traditional attires from different regions showcasing our heritage while we celebrated the visionaries shaping the Nigerian business landscape. We honored the spirit of enterprise through our symbolic “tribes” — Dangote , Otedola , Elumelu and Cubana — representing the strength, resilience, and innovation that define Nigerian business excellence.

The night also belonged to remarkable entrepreneurs who continue to inspire and redefine success:

  • GGLITZ Brand — Entrepreneur of the Year
  • Bloomwoman Fashion Store — Entrepreneur of the Year
  • Lady B Fabrics — Entrepreneur of the Year
  • Stragib Integrated Ventures — Entrepreneur of the Year
  • NK Foods — Resilience Champion
  • Scent by Mildred Perfume — Trailblazer of the Year and Overall Business of the Year
  • Lady B Fabrics — Entrepreneur of the Year

It was more than an event — it was a powerful statement of culture, resilience, and entrepreneurial brilliance. Business Culture Fest 2025 reminded us that when business meets culture, the result is unforgettable.
