Meet the team

A small, dedicated team of community leaders, program managers, and volunteers who run events, and partnerships to help Asaba's Business Community thrive.

Our team combines local market experience with program design and operations expertise — focused on measurable outcomes for vendors.

Our culture & code of conduct

We are committed to respectful collaboration, accountability, and fair opportunity. Vendors and partners are expected to follow our vendor code of conduct designed to ensure safety, transparency, and equitable access.

  • Respectful behaviour towards vendors, customers, and staff.
  • Transparent pricing and clear stall accountability.
  • Safety and cleanliness in all event spaces.
  • Non-discrimination and inclusion in vendor selection and participation.
